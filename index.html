Our story

In 2020 the house of my step-parents was robbed. A lot of personal belongings was lost and we've realized that there is no evidence for them. No photos, so it is hard to describe them, hard to claim the case to the insurance company. SOme IT stuff was lost as well, but we had no serial number of them. That was the point when I've realized that something must be done. - Marek

After a while there were different priorities, COVID etc. But in 2022 our roofbox was stolen directly from my car. It was a wedding gift, so no invoice, and again, no photo, no serial number. Month after that we've lost our bikes. No it is not a joke, Prague is one of the safest places on the planet, but there are definitely some outliers... -Marek

Our main driver is to reduce the amount of stolen items, give a better chance to people to claim the insurance and set a bounty for their stuff. We help police to identify stolen items as well. We have 2022 and serial numbers are everywhere, lets spent some minutes and give you a chance to recover your items.
